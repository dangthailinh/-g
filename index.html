About the Charity Run

Charity Run 2024 aims to raise funds for the less fortunate in our community. Join us in this noble cause while enjoying a day of fun and excitement.

Program Events

Our charity run event includes a variety of activities that cater to all age groups and fitness levels.

  • 5km Family Run
  • 10km Competitive Run
  • Kids' Fun Run
  • Live Music Performances
  • Food and Drinks Stalls

General Information

Venue Details

The Charity Run 2024 will take place at Saigon Exhibition and Convention Center (SECC), Hochiminh City.

Transportation and Parking

  • Free parking is available for all participants.
  • Bus routes: 38, 72, and 102 will stop near the venue.
